User Scenario Mapping

Scenario

Sean in a junior in highschool, looking for cool shoes at a affordable price. Since Sean is a highschooler, he cannot drive very far to find small sneaker shops. Sean goes online to see his options and finds Sweet Soles. He picks out his most-wanted pair of Dunks, and happily awaits for their arrival.

Actor

Sean

Motivator

Wanting a fresh pair of shoes.

Intention

Wanting affordable prices on high-end shoes and is limited to where he can drive.

Action

Sean goes online and finds a reliable sneakerhead store. He looks through the brands offered and ensures the legitimacy of the product by reading the reviews.

Resolution

Sean finds his dream shoe at a great price and orders it online.
